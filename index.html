Frequently Asked Questions

How does Tunexus provide free global radio streaming?

Tunexus operates on an ad-supported model. We partner with leading advertising networks to display relevant, high-quality ads. This revenue allows us to cover our streaming infrastructure costs and keep the service completely free for all users worldwide, ensuring universal access to music and news.

Is my data safe while listening on Tunexus?

Yes, your privacy is our top priority. We do not collect personally identifiable information. We use anonymous usage data (like popular stations) to improve our service, and local storage on your device to save preferences like themes and favorites. Our platform is built with data privacy in mind.

Can I use Tunexus on multiple devices like mobile and desktop?

Absolutely. Tunexus is a Progressive Web App (PWA) designed for a seamless cross-device experience. It works perfectly in any modern browser on your desktop, tablet, or smartphone. For an app-like experience, you can add Tunexus to your home screen from your browser's menu.
